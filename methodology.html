Methodology

Data notes and source guidance

This page describes how the fictional accountability records are organized for the 2025-2026 Summit training release.

Current Release Scope

The portal covers ten fictional Summit Charter Schools campuses across Arizona. Campus profile pages use the current 2025-2026 report-card values from the shared campus performance data map.

The current release includes enrollment, overall letter grade, math proficiency, ELA proficiency, graduation rate when applicable, chronic absenteeism, and report source-page references.

Report Page References

Academic performance values are expected on report-card page 2. Attendance and enrollment indicators are expected on report-card page 3. These page references support later extraction and citation work.

Campuses that do not serve a full graduating cohort show graduation rate as Not Applicable.

Metric definitions

How to read the fields

Overall letter grade

A synthetic accountability rating for the current report year. It is not a state-issued grade.

Math and ELA proficiency

Percent of students meeting the fictional proficiency standard in each subject area.

Graduation rate

Shown for high school campuses that serve a graduating cohort. It is marked Not Applicable elsewhere.

Chronic absenteeism

Percent of students marked chronically absent in the synthetic attendance record.

Watch Signals

  • Chronic absenteeism above 20% is treated as an attendance watch signal.
  • Math or ELA proficiency below 30% is treated as an academic support signal.
  • D or F letter grades are highlighted for regional follow-up.
  • Profile narratives keep the tone operational and constructive.

Report Archive Notes

Current report-card links are posted on campus profile pages using stable paths. Prior-year archive rows are present to make the site feel like a public report archive, but prior-year documents are placeholders until an archive package is built.

Synthetic Data Disclaimer

All campuses, people, grades, metrics, report cards, URLs, and operational notes in this portal are fictional and synthetic. The site is for AI Challenge Week 2 training and should not be interpreted as a real school accountability system.

Summit Charter Schools is fictional. This public accountability portal contains synthetic training data only.
